STAY

CULINARY

Culinary tourism also known as gastronomy tourism is the focus on food as an attraction for exploration and a destination for tourism. Although food has always been a part of hospitality services for tourists.

GUIDE

Guide is a member of the hospitality and travel industry who leads groups or individuals to tourist attractions. Tour guides offer information and insights to help visitors make the most of their experience.
